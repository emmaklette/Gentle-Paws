About Gentle Paws

Inspired by our beloved dog Olive, Gentle Paws is a wellness-focused brand dedicated to supporting dog parents with affordable, allergy-friendly, and thoughtful products. From eco-conscious dog care essentials to heartfelt digital education, we nurture the bond between dogs and their humans through clean, natural, and intentional living.

What We Offer

  • Allergy-free dog soaps & natural grooming
  • Clothing tailored for all breeds with allergy & temperature control
  • Hypoallergenic bedding & bowls
  • Dog hiking gear & portable water bottles
  • First aid kits for allergies & everyday needs (e.g. charcoal)
  • Educational blogs, social content & e-guides
  • Affordable physical products for pet wellness
